SFI Initiative

SFI Jadavpur University Engineering Unit

The Students' Federation of India (SFI) Engineering Unit at Jadavpur University is dedicated to assisting students through their academic journey. Every year, during the WBJEE counseling process, we run physical and digital helpdesks to guide students through form fill-up, document verification, and college/branch options.

Our mission is to stand by students and ensure that quality education remains democratic, accessible, and transparent.

Jadavpur University FET Facts

  • High Placement Rates: Top-tier multinational core engineering and IT firms visit the JU campus annually, boasting packages matching premier IITs.
  • Nominal Fee Structure: At approx ₹2,400 per year (and ₹30,000 per year for IT), Jadavpur University offers the highest return on investment (ROI) in India.
  • Research Culture: FET has top-of-the-line research labs, funding, and international paper publications led by highly qualified faculty.
